Overview

I am an imaginative person who likes engaging in creative pursuits, be it academic or extra curricular. The desire for originality makes me happy whenever I am able to come up with something new. This has manifested itself in my research and also in my creative expression such as composing poems, singing, graphics editing, photography, and blogging.

Research Interests

My research is motivated by a simple fact: There's more solar power hitting the Earth every hour than the global annual power consumption! Clearly, there's no dearth of energy resource in the world. The only challenge is: How do we develop technologies and systems that can effectively utilize this resource?
  • Solar Energy Technologies, Radiative transfer, Photonics, Plasmonics
  • Phase Change Heat Transfer, Energy Systems, Nanotechnology
