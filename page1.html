Overview

The desire for originality makes me happy whenever I am able to do something creative. This has manifested itself in my various endeavours like composing poems, music, graphics editing etc. Being involved in research work, I have realised that the lessons learned through these activities apply to research as well. Thus, in my opinion, research is a very creative process.

The prospect of doing innovative work excites me, knowing that research and innovations have been the driving force behind all the comforts that we enjoy today.

Research Interests

  • Solar Energy, Phase Change Heat Transfer, Energy Systems, Thermal Management
  • Thermochemistry, Energy storage systems, Nanotechnology, Phase Change Materials

Other Interests and Activities

Travelling

















Travelling has been one of the biggest sources of learning for me. I am amazed at how each travel is a different experience and how much there is in this world to explore. The beauty of nature mesmerises me and makes me forget all my worries. Till date, I have managed to travel to various parts of India, from the mighty Himalayas in the North, to beaches of Visakhapattanam in the South, and from the Thar Desert in Western Rajasthan, to the North-Eastern beauty that is Sikkim.
